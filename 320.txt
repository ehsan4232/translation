CHAPTER 12: DESIGN A CHAT SYSTEM In this chapter we explore the design of a chat system. Almost everyone uses a chat app. Figure 12-1 shows some of the most popular apps in the marketplace. A chat app performs different functions for different people. It is extremely important to nail down the exact requirements. For example, you do not want to design a system that focuses on group chat when the interviewer has one-on-one chat in mind. It is important to explore the feature requirements.
