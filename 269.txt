CHAPTER 10: DESIGN A NOTIFICATION SYSTEM A notification system has already become a very popular feature for many applications in recent years. A notification alerts a user with important information like breaking news, product updates, events, offerings, etc. It has become an indispensable part of our daily life. In this chapter, you are asked to design a notification system. A notification is more than just mobile push notification.
