• Transcoded storage: It is a blob storage that stores transcoded video files. • CDN: Videos are cached in CDN. When you click the play button, a video is streamed from the CDN. • Completion queue: It is a message queue that stores information about video transcoding completion events. • Completion handler: This consists of a list of workers that pull event data from the completion queue and update metadata cache and database. Now that we understand each component individually, let us examine how the video uploading flow works. The flow is broken down into two processes running in parallel. a. Upload the actual video. b. Update video metadata. Metadata contains information about video URL, size, resolution, format, user info, etc. Flow a: upload the actual video
